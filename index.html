Our Story

Modesty is
beauty

Noor Hijab was born from a simple belief — that every woman deserves to wear her faith with elegance and ease. Based in the heart of Kinondoni, Dar es Salaam, we handpick every piece with love.

From flowing khimars to graceful abayas and beautiful dresses, each item is chosen for its quality, softness, and style. We are here for the modest girls — past, present, and future.
